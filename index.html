Mission Statement

The Boston University Hindu Cultural Association aims to promote awareness of the Hindu religion and to foster a greater understanding of Hindu heritage among the BU community. We strive to enrich the lives of students studying here by providing a platform through which they can freely practice, discuss, and learn more about Hinduism. We also aim to raise awareness about social and religious issues affecting Hindus in the 21st century. BU HCA also aims to utilize available resources to help communities in need through humanitarian efforts of all sorts.
